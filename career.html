Frequently Asked Questions

What is the admission procedure?

The admission process involves submitting an online application form, followed by document verification and an interaction session for higher classes. Please visit our Admissions page for more details.

Is transportation available for all areas?

Yes, we have a fleet of GPS-enabled AC buses covering almost all major routes in the city. You can check specific routes at our transport office.

What co-curricular activities are offered?

We offer a wide range of activities including Sports (Cricket, Football, Basketball), Performing Arts (Music, Dance, Drama), Fine Arts, Robotis, and Debating.
